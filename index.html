What We Do

SELECT is a professional interview preparation and career consultation platform. We conduct structured one-on-one mock interviews, interview simulations, and personalised guidance sessions for students and early professionals preparing for academic, government, and private-sector selection processes.

How It Works

  1. Candidates submit an initial interest form
  2. Each submission is reviewed based on relevance and urgency
  3. Shortlisted candidates are contacted with next steps
  4. Sessions are conducted in a structured interview format

Session Details

  • One-on-one online interaction
  • 45 minutes interview simulation or counselling discussion
  • 15 minutes structured feedback and guidance
  • Total Session Duration: 60 minutes

Who Should Apply

  • Students preparing for competitive exams or academic selections
  • Early professionals preparing for interviews or role transitions
  • Candidates seeking structured evaluation, mentoring, or career clarity

Begin the Application Process

SELECT follows a review-based onboarding process. Interested candidates are required to submit their details through an initial interest form.

Submitting this form does not guarantee a session. Applications are reviewed based on preparation stage, urgency, and current availability.

Shortlisted candidates will be contacted separately with further instructions regarding session format, scheduling, and next steps.
